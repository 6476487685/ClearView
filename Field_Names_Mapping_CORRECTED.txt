CRITICAL FIELD NAMING RULE:
────────────────────────────
Certain fields are COMMON across Expense, Income, and Investment modules.
These fields should NOT have module prefixes/suffixes:

COMMON FIELDS (No prefix/suffix):
- Payment_Mode     (NOT Expense_Mode, Income_Mode, Investment_Mode)
- Txn_Tag          (NOT Expense_Txn_Tag, Income_Txn_Tag)
- Ac_Status        (NOT Expense_Account_Status, Income_Account_Status)
- Txn_Status       (NOT Expense_Txn_Status, Income_Txn_Status)
- Frequency        (NOT Expense_Frequency, Income_Frequency)

MODULE-SPECIFIC FIELDS (Should have module prefix):
- Expense_Description, Expense_Category, Expense_Tag, Expense_Currency, Expense_Amount
- Income_Description, Income_Category, Income_Tag, Income_Currency, Income_Amount
- Investment_Description, Investment_Category, Investment_Tag, Investment_Currency, Investment_Amount
