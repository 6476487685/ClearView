These fields should be used WITHOUT module prefix across ALL modules:

✅ 1. Ac_Holder          - Account holder name

✅ 3. Payment_Mode       - Payment method (Bank, Cash, etc.)
✅ 4. Frequency           - Frequency of transaction
✅ 5. Amount              - Transaction amount
✅ 6. Ac_Status           - Account status (Active, Inactive)
✅ 7. Txn_Status          - Transaction status (Paid, Unpaid, etc.)
✅ 8. Due_Date            - Due date (used in Expense & Investment)

✅ 10. Invest_Date        - Investment date (used in Investment)

CONFIRMATION - FIELD NAME RULES
================================================================================

✅ FIELD NAMING RULES:

1. SHARED FIELDS (No Prefix):
   - Used across Expense, Income, Investment modules
   - Examples: Ac_Holder, Txn_Tag, Payment_Mode, Frequency, etc.

2. MODULE-SPECIFIC FIELDS (With Prefix):
   - Expense: Expanse_Description, Expanse_Category, Expanse_Ac_Tag
   - Income: Income_Description, Income_Category, Income_Ac_Tag
   - Investment: Investment_Description, Investment_Category, Investment_Ac_Tag
   - Task: Task_Description, Task_Category, Task_Tag, etc.

3. DATE FIELDS:
   - Due_Date (Expense, Investment)
   - Paid_Date (Expense only)
   - Invest_Date (Investment only)
   - Income_Date (Income only)

4. CROSS-REFERENCE FIELDS:
   - Paid_From (Links Expense to Income_Ac_Tag)
